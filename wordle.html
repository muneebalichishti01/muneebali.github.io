WORDLE

Rules

  • 1. The game is played on a grid that has 4 letter word. You will be given a hidden word.
  • 2. The objective of the wordle is to find all the correct letters of that word.
  • 3. Letters will be placed horizontally on the grid.
  • 4. Letters can be removed by using BACKSPACE.
  • 5. Start by typing any letter. Then hit ENTER.
  • 6. Letters with green background are in the right spot.
  • 7. Letters with yellow background exist in the word, but are in wrong box.
  • 8. Letters with gray background do not exist in the word.
  • 9. If you need a hint, click the ? icon.
  • 10. When you find all the letters, you win!
